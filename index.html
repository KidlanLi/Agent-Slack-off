Medical History Documentation

In his consultation with medical professionals, Zixuan reported the following symptoms and experiences:

Academic and Professional Challenges

Zixuan told his doctor that he had experienced frequent academic delays and time management difficulties since his college days. Even today, after many years of working, he still cannot complete tasks on time. Although he has been with the company for five years, his performance evaluations are often marked down for "inattention," "lack of organization," and "inability to meet deadlines."

Attention Difficulties

The most common manifestation was that he was extremely distracted. Whenever he is in a work mode, he is often interrupted by conversations with colleagues, cell phone notifications, or even the view from the window. Even in important meetings, he has difficulty concentrating on listening to the entire briefing, often answering questions or missing information on key issues. He often forgets to respond to emails, misses schedules, and even leaves important documents at home.

Organizational Challenges

Zixuan exhibited severe organizational difficulties. His desk was perennially cluttered, with mountains of papers stacked on top of each other. He tried to use notebooks, apps or calendars to remind himself to organize tasks, but they never lasted more than a few days. The progress of many projects was seriously delayed due to his "ad-hoc" approach, even affecting teamwork.

Impulse Control Issues

In addition to attention problems, impulse control disorder also plagued him. He often interrupts others in discussions with his colleagues, and even blurts out his own ideas when his supervisor is speaking, leading to a perception that he lacks respect and patience in the team. He also has a problem with impulsive spending. Knowing that his credit card limit is tight, he will still make large online purchases on a whim and then regret it afterwards.

Restlessness and Sleep Issues

Zixuan also exhibits a constant inner restlessness. He is unable to relax on weekends, and even when he sits on the couch, he unconsciously shakes his legs, bites his fingernails, or changes TV programs frequently. He described himself as having "endless thoughts" in his head, especially at night before going to sleep, which caused him to suffer from chronic insomnia.

Self-Adjustment Attempts

He has tried to self-adjust his lifestyle to "become normal," such as forcing himself to get up early and making a list of daily tasks, but these methods often fall flat after a few days. He had considered whether he had an anxiety disorder or depression, but after several interviews, the counselor found that he had been characterized by inattentive and impulsive behaviors since he was a child, such as forgetting his homework in elementary school, interrupting the teacher in class, receiving inconsistent grades, and frequently changing seats to "keep him in check."

Impact on Personal Life

Today, Zixuan tells his doctor that his ADHD symptoms have affected his career, marital relationships and self-identity. He is often seen by his wife as "irresponsible" and "untrustworthy", and his interactions with his children are often conflictual due to emotional instability. On many occasions, he felt frustrated, blamed himself, and wondered if he had been born with a "missing link".
